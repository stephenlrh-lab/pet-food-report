# 宠物食品动态报告 Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

专业市场情报简报，面向宠物食品工厂和产品决策者。视觉应兼具食品行业的温暖感、全球贸易的理性秩序、研发创新的清晰数据感。页面不是营销落地页，而是一个可浏览、可汇报、可继续更新的动态研究看板。

## Colors

- Ink: `#17201c` for primary text.
- Paper: `#fbfaf6` for background.
- Panel: `#ffffff` for cards and tables.
- Leaf: `#2f7d5c` for health and functional nutrition.
- Amber: `#d99a2b` for opportunity and commercial signals.
- Ocean: `#2367a2` for global/regional data.
- Berry: `#a83f5f` for risk and consumer pain points.

## Typography

- Font stack: `Inter`, `Noto Sans SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, `Arial`, sans-serif.
- Numeric tables use tabular numerals.
- Headings are compact and information-dense; no oversized marketing hero typography after the first viewport.

## Motion

- Entrance motion should be calm and executive: small y-shifts, opacity fades, restrained scale.
- No random animation.
- Section reveals happen when scrolling.
- Data cards may use subtle stagger, but never block reading.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use a single-hue palette.
- Do not use floating decorative orbs or bokeh.
- Do not hide the actual report behind a marketing hero.
- Do not make text overlap with cards or charts.
- Do not use vague claims without source links.
